Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods.Hereis a receipt for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ltbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gentlyu mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spactula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (itwill continue to cook on the plae unitl served)
